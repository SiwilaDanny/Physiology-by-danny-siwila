# GI Physiology Challenge Game

An interactive quiz game to test your knowledge of gastrointestinal physiology.

## How to Play
- Answer multiple-choice questions about GI physiology
- Track your score and progress
- Three game modes: Quick Quiz, Full Exam, and Organ-Specific Quiz

## Topics Covered
- GI anatomy and layers
- Nerve supply (ENS, sympathetic, parasympathetic)
- Saliva formation and regulation
- Gastric secretion phases
- Pancreatic function
- Liver and gallbladder physiology
- Intestinal absorption
- GI motility
- Gastrointestinal hormones
